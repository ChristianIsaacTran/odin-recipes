Baked Salmon Dijon

Description

An easy-to-bake salmon recipe that is coated with a delicious Dijon mustard glaze and seasoned breadcrumbs drizzled with butter.

Ingredients

  • 4 (4 ounce) fillets of salmon
  • 3 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup italian-style dry bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of butter, melted
